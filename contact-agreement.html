Business Rules

To ensure a smooth and efficient loan application process, we have established a set of business rules. These rules are designed to maintain clarity, accuracy, and fairness for all applicants. Adhering to these guidelines will help streamline your application and enhance the likelihood of approval.

  1. Unique Borrower ID : Each applicant must be assigned a unique Borrower ID to ensure distinct identification within the system.
  2. Mandatory Fields : All fields within the borrower information form are mandatory, with exceptions granted for optional sections pertaining to spouse, children, or dependents.
  3. Purpose Statement : Applicants are required to provide a clear and concise statement detailing the purpose of their loan request, ensuring alignment with their intended use of the funds.
  4. Single Borrower Per Loan : Applicants can make multiple loan requests, but each loan request must have exactly one borrower associated with it. This prevents duplicated loans and ensures that each applicant's requests are uniquely identified and processed accurately.
  5. Collateral Option : Applicants may provide one collateral, which can be used to secure their loan application, providing additional assurance for the lender. While providing collateral is optional, it serves to strengthen the loan application and increase the likelihood of approval.
  6. Existing Loans : Applicants have the option to provide details of one or more existing loans.
  7. Financial Disclosure: Applicants must disclose their financial information. They may include financial information for their spouse to strengthen their loan eligibility. However, they are only required to provide their own employment status and total income. Each financial record should be linked to only one borrower.
  8. Deposit Accounts: Applicants must provide at least one or more deposit accounts.
